What is MyGate?

An app that simplifies life for everyone in a gatedcommunity, from residents and management committee members to security guards and facility managers. Packed with features, it reduces many hassles—authorising entry of delivery executives, paying maintenance bills, raising a ticket to the facility manager—to a single click.




MyGate for every need





Security Management

Ensure that every person, package or vehicle entering
the community is authorised by a resident. Effective
security measures can be convenient, too!

Community Management

Manage accounts and payments, resolve complaints
and keep the community up-to-date with all that’s
going on in the society. Easy for the management
committee and residents.
